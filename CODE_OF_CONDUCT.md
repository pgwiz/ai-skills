# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our standards

We expect all contributors to adhere to the Contributor Covenant v2.1 code of conduct: be respectful, collaborative, and inclusive.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, abusive language, doxxing, or any form of discrimination.

Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a private issue or contacting the project maintainers. Maintainers will review reports and respond according to the severity of the incident.

Contact

If you are reporting an incident, please contact the maintainers via the repository's issue tracker (set the issue privacy appropriately) or the contact listed in repository settings.
